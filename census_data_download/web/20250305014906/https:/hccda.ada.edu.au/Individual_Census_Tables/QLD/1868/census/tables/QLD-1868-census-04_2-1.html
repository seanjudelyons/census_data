CLASSIFICATION OF OCCUPATIONS — CENSUS OF 1868 —continued.
GENERAL HEADS. SUBDIVISIONS AND EQUIVALENTS.
XVI. Farmers. Farm Proprietors employing men, Persons engaged in Agricultural and Horticultural Pursuits, Vegetable Gardeners.
XVII. Farm and Domestic Servants. Males—Farm Servants employed on hire, Coachmen, Grooms, Horsebreakers.Females—Servants of all kinds in hired Domestic Service.
XVIII. Domestic Duties. Females employed in Household Duties, not hired.
XIX. Mariners. Masters, Pilots, Officers, Stevedores, Seamen, Boatmen, Ballastmen, Lightermen, Sea Cooks, Stewards, Cabin Boys, Sea Fishermen, Divers, Lumpers.
XX. Miscellaneous Occupations. Actors and Actresses, Cabmen, Carriers, Collectors, Dressmakers, Mattress Makers, Proprietors of Registry Offices, Tollkeepers, Toymen, Undertakers.
XXI. Persons supported at the Public Expense and Gratuitously. Paupers, Criminals, and Lunatics.
XXII. Unspecified. Cases where no Occupation was stated, or where it was impossible to classify the nature of the Employment under any of the previous heads.
